About Me

Fresh Data Scientist with +5 years of experience in eCommerce and Marketing across multiple industries. From mass consumption giants such as Coca-Cola and SC Johnson to two of the biggest eCommerce companies of Latin America (PedidosYa -part of the Delivery Hero holding-, and Mercado Libre, the biggest marketplace of the continent) and traditional retail. Being passionate about research, I decided to pivot my career path and enrol myself on the Data Science Immersive at General Assembly. I am now looking for a role where I can combine my brand new Data Science skills with my expertise understanding the underlying reasons for users behaviour and finding data-driven insights.

Experience

General Assembly

Data Science Immersive | Full Time Programme

420 hours of professional training, hands-on application of machine learning, natural language, recommendation systems and other concepts. Working with tools such as SQL or Web Scraping across individual and group projects, presenting results to technical and non-technical audiences. My final project was about predicting current value of football players. Find more about my final project in this GitHub repository.

Mercado Libre

Head of Marketing | Uruguay & Perú

Mercado Libre is Latin America’s biggest marketplace with +249 million users. As the Marketing country lead, my role was to maintain and improve Mercado Libre’s leadership position in Uruguay, while developing Peru’s market. During my time in the role, the company improved its position as Top of Mind brand in Uruguay, improving its knowledge from 44% to 52% -followed by Amazon with 18%-. In addition, my team reached a traffic growth of +15% YoY in Uruguay -being the 4th most visited website, only behind Google, Facebook and YouTube- and +40% in Perú, while obtaining campaign ROIs above 30pts.
